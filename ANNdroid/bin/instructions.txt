1) Complete student profile before you play your first game.

2) Upon clicking the play button, a map displaying different subjects will be shown.

3) Choose the corresponding subject that you want to take by clicking its icon.

4) You will engage in a quiz bee type of game with an AI.

5) Defeat the opponent by answering the series of questions correctly. Every mistake costs a single life.

6) Game will end when a player loses all of his lives or if all questions are already asked. If all questions were asked, player with the most number of remaining lives wins.
